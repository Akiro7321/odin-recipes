Pizza

Description

Pizza is a round baked salty food with various toppings

Ingredients

  • 2 flour
  • 1 water
  • 5 yeast
  • 1 tomato sauce
  • 1 toppings

Steps

  1. mix water and flour to make a dough
  2. beat up the dough until it's a ball
  3. stretch it out to a pizza shape
  4. spread tomato sauce on it
  5. add toppings and mozzarella
  6. preheat oven to 250
  7. leave in oven for 25 min
