Sushi

Description

sushi is a Japanese dish of prepared vinegared rice

usually with some sugar and salt, accompanied by a variety of ingredients

Ingredients

  • rice
  • seaweed
  • sake
  • tamago
  • goma

Steps

  1. put rice on seaweed
  2. add ingredients
  3. roll the nori
  4. let it rest for a bit
  5. slice the roll into thin cylinders
